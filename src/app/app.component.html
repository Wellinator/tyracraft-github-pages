New Features

Some features that are currently working in TyraCraft. Tutorial coming soon!

Custom Music

Now you can load your favorite songs and listen to then at main menu or during the game play.

Take a look at the README in assets/menu/sounds and assets/game/creative

Texture Packs

You can create, load and share texture packs dynamically.

Take a look at the README in assets/textures/texture_packs

Save Game

You can save and load your worlds to play later or share with friends. The world are saved at saves folder at root of you assets directory

The worlds are being saved on USB only temporarily
